# 🚀 Helio WellnessApp - Marketing Launch Plan

## 1. App Store Optimization (ASO)

### App Name
**Helio Wellness AI: Coach & Food Scanner**

### Short Description
Scan food, track your health, and chat with your personal AI Wellness Coach.

### Long Description
Welcome to Helio Wellness, your all-in-one AI health companion! 

**Key Features:**
- 🤖 **AI Wellness Coach**: Powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash, ask anything about your diet, health, and fitness goals.
- 🍏 **Smart Food Scanner**: Simply take a photo of your meal to instantly get nutritional info and logging.
- ⚔️ **Social Battles**: Challenge friends to see who can smash their fitness goals faster!
- 📈 **Full Offline Support**: Your data syncs securely with Firebase and is available completely offline via Capacitor storage.

Get started for free or upgrade to Premium for Unlimited AI Coaching!
