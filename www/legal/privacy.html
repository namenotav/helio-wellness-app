PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

Our Commitment: WellnessAI is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal health information with the highest standards of security and confidentiality.

1. Information We Collect

Health Information

  • Step count and physical activity data
  • Food and nutrition logs
  • Health metrics (weight, height, BMI)
  • GPS location (only when tracking activities)
  • Device sensor data (accelerometer, gyroscope)
  • Photos uploaded for food analysis
  • DNA data (optional, if uploaded by user)

Account Information

  • Email address and name
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Device identifier
  • Date of birth (optional)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide step tracking and health monitoring
  • Generate AI-powered health recommendations
  • Analyze food and nutrition
  • Track your progress toward health goals
  • Send notifications about your health activities
  • Improve the App and fix bugs
  • Provide customer support
We DO NOT:
  • Sell your health data to third parties
  • Share identifiable health information without consent
  • Use your data for advertising

3. Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive health data
  • HTTPS for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with Firebase
  • Regular security audits
  • HIPAA and GDPR compliance measures

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out of optional data collection
  • Withdraw consent at any time

5. Data Retention

  • Active account data: Retained while account is active
  • After deletion: Data permanently removed within 30 days
  • Backup data: Removed within 90 days
  • Legal requirements: May retain for compliance purposes

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services:

  • Firebase - Cloud storage and authentication
  • Google Gemini AI - Health analysis (anonymized data)
  • Stripe - Payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • ElevenLabs - Voice guidance (optional feature)

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (EU)
  • Privacy Shield certification (US)
  • GDPR-compliant data processing agreements

8. Children's Privacy

WellnessAI is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy
  • Material changes will be notified via email or app notification
  • Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance
