# PRIVACY POLICY

**Last Updated: November 30, 2025**

## Our Commitment to Your Privacy

WellnessAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal health information with the highest standards of security and confidentiality.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Health Information
- Step count and physical activity data
- Food and nutrition logs
- Health metrics (weight, height, BMI)
- GPS location (only when tracking activities)
- Device sensor data (accelerometer, gyroscope)
- Photos uploaded for food analysis
- DNA data (optional, if uploaded by user)

### Account Information
- Email address
- Name
- Password (encrypted)
- Device identifier
- Date of birth (optional)

### Usage Information
- App feature usage
- Error logs and crash reports
- Analytics data (anonymous)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide step tracking and health monitoring
- Generate AI-powered health recommendations
- Analyze food and nutrition
- Track your progress toward health goals
- Send notifications about your health activities
- Improve the App and fix bugs
- Provide customer support

**We DO NOT:**
- Sell your health data to third parties
- Share identifiable health information without consent
- Use your data for advertising
- Access your data without encryption

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### Encryption
- All health data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- Passwords are hashed using secure one-way algorithms
- Data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption

### Storage Location
- Primary storage: On your device (encrypted localStorage)
- Cloud backup: Optional, encrypted before upload
- Server storage: Minimal, only for AI processing (deleted after use)

### Data Retention
- Active account: Data retained while account is active
- Deleted account: All data permanently deleted within 30 days
- Backups: Removed from backups within 90 days

---

## 4. Data Sharing

### We Share Data ONLY In These Cases:

**With Your Consent:**
- Insurance companies (only if you opt-in for rewards program)
- Healthcare providers (only if you choose to share)
- Emergency contacts (only during emergencies)

**For Legal Reasons:**
- Court orders or legal requirements
- Protecting rights and safety
- Fraud prevention

**Service Providers:**
- Google Gemini AI (for health coaching, data anonymized)
- Cloud hosting (Railway, data encrypted)
- Analytics (anonymous, no personal identifiers)

**We NEVER share your health data for marketing or advertising purposes.**

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA Compliant)

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data we have about you
- **Export**: Download your complete health data
- **Delete**: Request complete deletion of your data
- **Correct**: Update inaccurate information
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics and tracking
- **Portability**: Transfer data to another service

**To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@wellnessai.app**

## 10. AI and Machine Learning

- We send anonymized health questions to Gemini AI

- Gemini does not store your conversations
- AI processing happens in real-time and data is not retained

**Local AI:**
- Pattern recognition runs on your device
